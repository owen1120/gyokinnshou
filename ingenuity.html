Breeding
Process

From the Embrace of the Ocean to Your Dining Table

The Breeding Process of Our Mullet Roe Brand

In our mullet roe brand, the breeding process is the cornerstone of product quality. It begins with selecting the finest mullets, ensuring each fish comes from clean and healthy marine areas. We collaborate with experienced fishermen who follow traditional fishing techniques while integrating modern scientific management methods to ensure the health and sustainability of the fish stock.

During the breeding process, we pay special attention to the mullets' living environment, ensuring the purity of the water and strictly controlling the quality and quantity of their feed. Our goal is to simulate the natural growth conditions of mullets in the wild, allowing them to develop in the most natural state, thus preserving the freshness and nutritional value of their flesh.

When the mullets reach the appropriate age for harvesting, we proceed under the guidance of experts. After harvesting, the processing of mullet roe begins immediately to maintain its optimal freshness and quality. Throughout the entire breeding process, we adhere to our respect for nature and commitment to quality, ensuring that our mullet roe is not only delicious but also environmentally and ecologically friendly.

Pickling
Process

Traditional Craftsmanship, Modern Flavor

The Art of Pickling Mullet Roe

The pickling process of our mullet roe brand is an art that perfectly blends traditional craftsmanship with modern technology. This process starts with the selection of raw materials—fresh, high-quality mullet roe, ensuring precision and quality in every step.

Before pickling, each piece of mullet roe is meticulously cleaned to remove impurities, ensuring its purity. Then, we apply a unique salt-curing formula, carefully developed through years of research and experimentation, designed to enhance the natural flavor of the mullet roe. Our salting process aims not only to preserve but also to enrich the taste and texture of the mullet roe.

Temperature and humidity control is crucial during the salting process. We use precise control systems to ensure that the mullet roe is cured in the most ideal conditions, maintaining its unique texture and aroma. The duration of the curing process is adjusted based on the size and thickness of the mullet roe to achieve the best flavor.

Finally, the cured mullet roe is carefully packaged to maintain its freshness and quality. Throughout the entire pickling process, we continuously pursue innovation while also preserving the traditional art of pickling, bringing you a taste experience that is both classic and modern.

Solar Drying
Process

Naturally Sun-Dried, Enhanced Flavor

Mullet Roe Under the Sun

In our mullet roe brand, the solar drying process is a crucial step in imparting a unique flavor to the product. This step maximizes the power of nature, slowly sun-drying the mullet roe to preserve its natural taste and nutrients.

The drying process begins with the selection of suitable sunny weather, ensuring that each drying session occurs under optimal natural conditions. The mullet roe is spread out on specially designed drying racks, which are made to ensure proper air circulation and to prevent direct, intense sunlight, thereby maintaining the quality of the mullet roe.

Throughout the drying process, controlling temperature and humidity is vital. We closely monitor these conditions to ensure that the mullet roe does not become overly dry or moist during the drying. This slow drying method helps the mullet roe develop a unique texture and aroma.

After the drying is complete, the mullet roe undergoes a series of quality inspections to ensure each piece meets our strict standards. Through this traditional and natural drying method, our mullet roe not only maintains its purest flavor but also brings a combination of health and deliciousness to you.

Blending Tradition with Culinary Innovation

Exclusive Creations: Original Recipes from Our Mullet Roe Store

Our mullet roe brand places great emphasis on developing original recipes to provide our customers with unique culinary experiences. These recipes combine traditional cooking techniques with modern, innovative ideas, adding new dimensions to the rich flavors of mullet roe.

A signature original recipe is "Mullet Roe with Apple Mustard Sauce." This dish ingeniously pairs the savory taste of mullet roe with the fresh spiciness of apple mustard sauce, creating an astonishing palate experience. Another innovative recipe is "Mullet Roe Pasta," where thin strips of mullet roe are added to traditional pasta, bringing a delightful marine flavor.

We also feature "Mullet Roe Rice," where finely chopped mullet roe is mixed into steaming hot white rice, seasoned with soy sauce and green onions—a simple yet flavorful dish. These recipes not only showcase the versatility of mullet roe but also fuse traditional and modern cuisine, offering our customers unprecedented culinary inspiration.
